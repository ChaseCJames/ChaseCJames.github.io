Research

Research Areas

My work spans mathematical modeling, microbial ecology, and fisheries — united by a quantitative approach to understanding marine ecosystems.

Modeling & Evolution

ecoTRACE

Developing ecoTRACE, a cellular quota-based phytoplankton model that incorporates stochastic evolutionary dynamics to understand how physiology and adaptation shape community composition in a changing ocean.
